Welcome to the Autobahn Map Viewer

Your go-to tool for exploring real-time Autobahn data in Germany. Navigate with ease and stay updated on key road conditions.

What is the Autobahn Map Viewer?

The Autobahn Map Viewer is a dynamic web-based dashboard that provides up-to-date information on Germany’s Autobahn network. It’s designed for travelers, truck drivers, and anyone interested in real-time road conditions. From service stations to road closures, this interactive tool gives you everything you need to make informed decisions while on the road.

Key Features

  • Interactive Map: Explore the Autobahn network on an interactive map powered by Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap. Zoom in, zoom out, and focus on specific regions with ease.
  • Real-Time Data: Get live updates on service stations, lorry parking, closures, warnings, and roadworks. Stay informed about traffic disruptions and essential facilities.
  • Customizable Map View: Toggle different data layers on and off, allowing you to focus on the information most relevant to your trip.
  • Clickable Markers: Interact with map markers for detailed information about each location, such as the status of service stations, closures, and more.
  • Legend & Map Key: Easily understand what each icon and color on the map represents with a clear and concise legend.
